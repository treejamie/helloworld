    The Introvert server enjoys quiet and prefers silence.

    It will be polite about requesting silence, up to a point, but after that the introvert may get quite forceful about matters. The introvert has a finite temper and may get "propery radgy" (geordie english).

    The introvert server always apologises for getting mad, and will clean up any mess that it has created. It doesn't hold a grudge but it also isn't very clever.

    The introvert only speaks English.
