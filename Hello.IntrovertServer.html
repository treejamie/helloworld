    Hello.IntrovertServer (HelloWorld - Elixir v1.0.0)

    View Source

    The Introvert server enjoys peace and quiet but has limited patience for interruptions.

    When greeted (:hello), the introvert politely requests silence up to a point, gradually escalating in frustration. If pushed too far (state reaches 8), it flips tables, terminates the Extrovert server, and enjoys a period of blissful silence. After a cooldown, the introvert feels regret and restarts the Extrovert server.

    State

    • Integer count representing the number of times the introvert has been disturbed.

    Behavior

    • Politely asks for quiet until its temper threshold is reached.
    • Shuts down the Extrovert server at peak frustration.
    • Waits before restarting Extrovert after calming down.
